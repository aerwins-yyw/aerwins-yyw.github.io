Data Science Master's Thesis

Smart City Policy Impact Modeling

Measured whether the ASEAN Smart Cities Network causally improved urban development outcomes, not just correlated with them, a harder causal-inference problem than typical regression. Grounded in Schumpeter's theory of innovation, which frames smart city initiatives as drivers of growth through technological and institutional innovation and entrepreneurship. Applied the Generalized Synthetic Control Method in R, building a synthetic counterfactual for each treated municipality from panel data spanning 500+ municipalities, including 507 non-member cities and regencies as control units, to isolate the network's effect from local trends and confounders. Found that member cities' economic output rose 16.94% four years after implementation (ATE = 0.1462, 95% CI: 0.1268–0.1656), a result placebo tests confirmed as robust.

Machine Learning Kompas R&D

2024 Indonesia Regional Election Electability Prediction

Predicted the win probability of 1,553 candidates across all 545 races in Indonesia's 2024 simultaneous regional elections (37 provinces, 415 regencies, 93 cities) for Kompas R&D — a scale with too few historical Indonesian elections to train on directly. Engineered features from candidate location, party coalition support, and local demographics, and used generative AI to synthetically expand the historical training set, producing per-candidate electability scores despite the data scarcity.

Machine Learning Harvard Capstone

Production-Grade Movie Recommendation Engine

Predicted how a user would rate a movie they hadn't seen — the core problem behind recommender systems — for the Harvard Data Science capstone, using a 10M-rating MovieLens subset (27,000 movies, 138,000+ users). Iterated from a simple average-rating baseline through movie/user bias terms to regularized matrix factorization that captures latent taste patterns, validating each step on a held-out test set to reach a final RMSE of 0.80.

Machine Learning Harvard Capstone

Red Wine Quality Classification

Classified Portuguese red wines as higher or lower quality from physicochemical properties alone (acidity, sulphates, alcohol, etc.), a common approach for automating part of quality-control testing. Built and compared Logistic Regression and KNN classifiers on both the raw imbalanced dataset and a balanced resample, then ran sensitivity/specificity trade-off analysis to choose a deployment threshold — reaching a best AUC of 0.880 (balanced KNN) and 0.858 (imbalanced Logistic Regression).

Machine Learning IBM Data Science Capstone

SpaceX Falcon 9 Landing Prediction

Estimated the cost advantage SpaceX holds from reusing Falcon 9 first stages, for the IBM Data Science capstone's scenario of pricing a competing launch bid. Collected launch data via the SpaceX REST API and web scraping, ran SQL-based statistics and geospatial analysis of launch sites, then built and compared logistic regression, SVM, decision tree, and KNN classifiers to predict first-stage landing success — the event that determines whether a booster can be reused.
